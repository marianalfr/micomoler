PAPERSEED

It is a simple machine that creates ecological seedbeds out of paper waste. It works using some sheets of paper mixed with water and pressing them together until they have dried. The result is a small paper plant pot where everybody can germinate some seeds that can be planted anywhere else to let them grow.
